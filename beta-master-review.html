Hikmah Quest Beta Review Guide

Use this page as the stable review link in the beta build. It gives testers one place to open the main flows, confirm legal/support pages, and send focused feedback.

بسم الله الرحمن الرحيم

Core Links

Home Realm selection and support panel. Mode Select Solo, team, difficulty, and answer-style setup. Team Hub Anonymous team room setup and conference testing. Gameplay Card flow, reveal logic, mobile dock, and feedback modal. Results Score summary and missed-card review screen. Beta Notes Participation, privacy, and reporting expectations.

What To Check

Beta links open without 404s from the home page and feedback modal.
Mobile gameplay controls stay reachable on narrow screens.
Name reveal, answer reveal, skip, and scoring actions behave correctly.
Results page reflects score, streak, missed cards, and team totals accurately.
Feedback copy, privacy wording, and legal links match the Hikmah Quest beta.

Feedback Format

Page or flow: home, mode select, gameplay, team hub, or results.
Realm and difficulty used when the issue happened.
Card prompt, answer, or Name of Allah if content is the problem.
Device and browser if the issue looks layout- or input-related.
Email reports to Donya with enough detail to reproduce the issue.
