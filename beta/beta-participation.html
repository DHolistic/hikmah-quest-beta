What Testers Should Expect

  • Features, decks, and visual behavior may change quickly during beta.
  • Some cards may still require corrections, citation tightening, or language polish.
  • Progress data may reset between revisions.

Tester Responsibilities

  • Report issues with enough context to reproduce.
  • Avoid redistributing unpublished beta builds or private test material without permission.
  • Use respectful, constructive feedback channels.

Feedback Scope

Feedback can include: content corrections, wrong references, wording improvements, and UX issues. Submission does not grant ownership over the project.

Offline, Online, and Communal Play

  • Offline play: supported after initial load and cache completion.
  • Online play: requires internet access for remote matchmaking and sync features (when enabled).
  • Nearby communal play (planned): may require Bluetooth and nearby-device permissions depending on platform.
  • AirDrop is not a gameplay transport requirement; it is a file-sharing feature on Apple devices.

Identity and Privacy in Conference Play

Round 2 team experiences are intended to support anonymous display names and optional hidden avatars for safer communal participation.
